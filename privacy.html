Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Upload Desk ("we", "our", "the app") is a planning and analytics tool for YouTube Shorts creators. This policy explains what information Upload Desk accesses, how it's used, and how it's stored.

1. Account data

When you create an Upload Desk account, we store your chosen username and a securely hashed version of your password (never the password itself) using Firebase Authentication. Your account data — including planner entries, ideas, goals, and settings — is stored in a private, per-account Firestore database record, accessible only to you when signed in.

2. Google Sign-In (website login)

You may optionally sign in to your Upload Desk account using "Continue with Google." This uses standard Google OAuth to verify your identity (name, email, profile picture) and does not grant Upload Desk access to any other Google data.

3. YouTube channel connection (optional, separate from your account)

Upload Desk lets you optionally connect your own YouTube channel to display live statistics. This is entirely separate from your Upload Desk account login above.

If you choose to connect a channel, you'll be asked to grant read-only access via Google OAuth to:

  • YouTube Data API (youtube.readonly) — public channel info, video list, and public statistics (views, likes, comments, subscriber count).
  • YouTube Analytics API (yt-analytics.readonly) — private, channel-owner-only metrics such as total watch time and average view percentage (retention).

Upload Desk never receives or stores your Google account password. Access tokens are kept only in your browser's memory for the current session and are not persisted to our servers. A snapshot of your channel's public statistics (name, avatar, subscriber count, view count, recent video list) is saved to your Upload Desk account so it can be displayed on your dashboard — this data is visible only to you.

You can disconnect your YouTube channel at any time from the YouTube tab in the app. Disconnecting revokes Upload Desk's access and does not affect your Upload Desk account or any other data.

4. Data storage and security

All account and planner data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore). Passwords are hashed before storage. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. Each user's data is isolated to their own account and is not accessible to other users.

5. Data you control

Within the app, you can export your data at any time (as JSON, CSV, or Excel), import a previous backup, or permanently erase all of your data from the Data tab. Deleted Shorts and ideas are moved to a Trash area first and can be restored, or permanently deleted at your discretion.

6. Third-party services used

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and data storage.
  • YouTube Data API & YouTube Analytics API (Google) — optional channel statistics, only if you choose to connect a channel.
  • Chart.js — used locally in your browser to render charts; no data is sent to a third party for this.
  • EmailJS — used only if you choose the email-verification method to link a YouTube channel, to deliver a one-time verification code to your inbox.

7. No advertising or data sale

Upload Desk does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes. There are no ads in the app.

8. Children's privacy

Upload Desk is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of Upload Desk after changes indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
