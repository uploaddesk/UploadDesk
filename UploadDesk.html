How Upload Desk works

Dashboard

A quick snapshot of the current month: how many Shorts are planned vs. uploaded, average 24-hour views, your upload streak, and your best/worst performing Short.

Planner

  • Click any day on the calendar (or a row in List view) to add a title, status, checklist, and performance numbers.
  • Day colors tell you its state — see the legend above the calendar: teal = completed, violet = planned, gold = today, red = missed, gray/locked = a past day with nothing added, which can no longer be edited.
  • Duplicate month copies every Short from the visible month into next month as fresh Plans (with performance numbers cleared). Reset month deletes everything for the visible month only.
  • Use the search box and filters to narrow the calendar or list down by title, status, or category.

Compare

Pick a comparison mode (two custom days, today vs. yesterday, this week vs. last week, or this month vs. last month) and press Compare to see views, likes, comments, shares, and subscriber gains side by side, plus the percentage difference.

Ideas

A backlog for Short ideas that aren't ready for a date yet. Give each one a title, notes, a content pillar, and tags, then press Send when you're ready — it creates a planned Short on the date you choose, carrying the title, notes, and category over automatically.

Goals & Achievements

Set a monthly upload count and total-views target and watch your progress bars fill in as the month goes. Below that, a 35-day streak heatmap and a grid of achievement badges (streaks, upload milestones, view milestones, and more) unlock automatically — no setup required.

Statistics

Longer-term numbers for a chosen month: total and average views, your most/least viewed Short, how consistently you uploaded, your best day of the week, your best-performing category, plus month-over-month growth and a simple next-month views forecast.

Notifications

The bell icon in the top bar flags things worth knowing: today's Short still isn't planned, yesterday's wasn't marked Uploaded, a Scheduled upload is coming up in the next two days, you're falling behind a monthly goal, or you've unlocked a new achievement. Dismiss any of them, or press Enable to also get a real browser notification for time-sensitive ones.

Data

Export a backup (JSON), a spreadsheet (CSV or Excel), import a previous JSON backup, or permanently erase everything. Everything also saves automatically to your account in the cloud as you work — these tools are for extra backups or moving your data to somewhere outside Upload Desk.

YouTube & TikTok

Optional — connect one platform (only one at a time) to replace manual Planner tracking with real, automatically-refreshed stats on the Dashboard, Compare, and Statistics screens. This is a separate sign-in from your Upload Desk account, using read-only access; disconnecting never affects your Upload Desk data. Once connected, the Planner becomes a personal notepad and no longer feeds those screens.

Your account

Your account and data are stored securely in the cloud and sync automatically. Log in with the same username and password on any browser or device to see the same data everywhere — changes made on one device show up on your others within moments. Use the account button in the sidebar to change your password, or "Log out" to switch accounts on this device. Export/Import in the Data tab is still there if you want a local backup file.
